What Are We

TOUCH A LIFE FOUNDATION (NGO) was founded in the year 2014 UNDER the banner of magus life. The main purpose of TOUCH A LIFE FOUNDATION was to promote the alleviation of human and animal suffering, by supporting selected activities of charitable, scientific, literary and educational nature as well as working with organizations and institutions in engaged activities.
